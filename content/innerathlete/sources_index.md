# InnerAthlete Source Index

This index summarizes the types of source material used to shape the product model without importing raw client material into the repo.

## Public-safe summaries

- Biomarker protocol notes
  - Sensitivity: `internal-reference-only`
  - Used for: sample timing, posture consistency, capillary collection context, and longitudinal interpretation notes.

- InnerAthlete example blood and DNA reports
  - Sensitivity: `do-not-port`
  - Used for: report structure, summary hierarchy, and action-plan framing.

- Biomarker and monitoring literature
  - Sensitivity: `public-safe`
  - Used for: longitudinal monitoring emphasis, recovery/load context, and caution against overreacting to single-point values.

- Genetics background documents
  - Sensitivity: `internal-reference-only`
  - Used for: domain grouping, non-deterministic caution language, and recommendation framing.

- S2-style cognition materials
  - Sensitivity: `internal-reference-only`
  - Used for: score-band structure, coaching translation, and readiness interaction notes.

## Never add directly to tracked repo content

- Named athlete reports
- Decrypted files
- Files labeled with names or initials
- Encryption keys or encrypted payloads
- Bundled raw report archives
- Raw S2 individual reports
- Source files containing direct identifiers
